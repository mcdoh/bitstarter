Plan your classes, manage your clients, and advertise your business with one service! Boxite will allow you to post your class schedule and size, allow your clients to sign up for classes, and announce special events. Boxite will also manage and bill your clients, and you won't have to deal with the laborious and time consuming PCI compliance procedures for processing credit cards. Best of all, boxite will serve your homepage and provide blogging and image posting services for you to advertise your business!

With Boxite, it's easy to add a client, punch in their credit card number, and set their rate. Beyond that, you can set them to a certain number of classes per month, or restrict them to certain types of classes. You can post pictures of your clients and your events. You can blog about upcoming events or improving technique. We will give you templates that you can use for your site from day one, you can tweak them to your liking, or you can create a brand new user experience from the ground up!

FAQ

Can I use my own domain name?
Sure! We can host your site for whichever domain names you control or host from example.boxite.com if you haven't chosen a domain name yet.
